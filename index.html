Data Mining and Precision Medical Laboratory

Where Teacher Zhou and his students do academic research and make progress together in Wuhan!

Welcome to the DMPM Lab!

The Data Mining and Precision Medical Laboratory of the School of Information, Huazhong Agricultural University, led by Associate Professor Zhou Xionghui. The laboratory mainly focuses on system biology and data mining.

Researches

Obtain the gene expression data of cancer patients through liquid biopsy and other technologies, and design a machine learning model to predict cancer.

Publications

The laboratory has published 18 academic papers, including 2 Class B conferences, 2 Class C journals, 12 SCI papers and 2 general papers.

Packages

The laboratory keeps gene expression data and drug sensitivity data of 8 cancers. The data were divided into liquid biopsy and tissue biopsy.
